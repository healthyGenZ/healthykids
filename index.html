Healthy Childhood Begins With Healthy Habits

Avoid Introducing Tea & Coffee to Kids

1. Parents - Think before introducing Tea or Coffee to kids

Tea and coffee contain caffeine which may affect sleep quality, digestion and the nervous system. Many adults experience acidity, heart palpitations and lack of deep sleep due to regular caffeine intake.Prevention is far easier than quitting later.

2. If We Drink, How Can We Tell Kids Not To?

Parents can honestly explain that certain health information was not widely known earlier, and they developed the habit unknowingly. Children understand honesty. Open communication builds trust and helps them choose healthier habits.

3. Lifetime Habit & Sugar Intake

Early introduction to tea or coffee often turns into a lifetime habit. These drinks usually include added sugar, increasing daily sugar intake. Reducing sugar later becomes difficult. It is better to protect children from developing this habit early in life.

4. Effects on Teeth & Nervous System

Tea and coffee may stain teeth and contribute to dental concerns over time. Caffeine stimulates the nervous system, potentially affecting sleep patterns, focus and emotional balance in children .
